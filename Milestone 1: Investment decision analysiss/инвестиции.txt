Stage 1 Analysis of investment decisions
These formulas show how to make calculations.
Total income =SUM(B3:B7)
Average income =AVERAGE(B3:B7)
NPV =NPV(10%,B3:B7)+B2
NPV (Net Present Value) is the net present value of the project, that is, the sum of all future cash flows discounted to the current moment, minus the initial investment.
Profitability Index =(NPV+1000000)/1000000
Risk (standard deviation) = STDEV(B3:B7)
The analysis of investment projects has shown that:
-projects J and G are the most effective, as they have the highest net present value (NPV) and a profitability index above 1.3
-these projects also have the shortest payback period.
-Project C is the least efficient
,therefore, Project J is the most preferable investment option.
